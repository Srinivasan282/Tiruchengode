Places to Visit

From hilltop vistas to tranquil dam sites and historic ashrams

Tiruchengode Hill Viewpoints

Stunning panoramic views of Namakkal district from the temple hill. Ideal for photography and quiet reflection, especially at dawn and dusk.

Gandhi Ashramam

A place of historical significance connected to the freedom movement. A serene spot for those interested in India's Gandhian heritage.

Theppa Ther

The Theppa Ther (Float Festival) at Tiruchengode is part of the 15-day annual Vaikasi Visakam festival for Arthanareeswarar Temple, featuring decorated floats for deities in the temple tank.

good People

The people of Tiruchengode are known for their deep-rooted cultural traditions, strong community bonds, and active involvement in local industries like power looms and education.

Local Markets

Experience authentic Tamil life at the town's markets. Shop for traditional items, spices, and handicrafts while soaking in local culture.
